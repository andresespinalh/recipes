Ingredients:
- 2 Eggs
- Salt (As wanted)
- Milk (As wanted)
- 2 spoons of sliced tomatoes
- 1 spoon of sliced chili
- 1 spoon of sliced onions
- 2 spoons of cheese

Directions:
  1. In a saucepan melt some butter
  2. On a bowl, stir the eggs, milk and salt until you get a firm mix
  3. Add the sliced tomatoes, chili and onions and stir constantly until cooked
  4. Add the cheese, until it melts
  5. Add the egg mix to the saucepan, let it cook on one side and then turn it over
